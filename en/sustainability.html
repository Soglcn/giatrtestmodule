Sustainability

Overview

At every stage of our integrated service approach, we embrace a sustainability-focused approach that supports growth and development. Our sustainability policy reflects our conscious commitment to fulfilling our responsibilities to society and nature by providing a comprehensive roadmap in every area of our operations.

Our Sustainability Approach: (Innovative Approach, Fair Society, Green Future)


Operating in accordance with strong ethical principles, we adhere to the best practices established in environmental, social, and corporate governance. Our management, compliance, health, and safety procedures have a contemporary, progressive, and professional character. Our organizational structure, experiences, effective governance policies, and clear processes enable us to undertake challenging and ambitious projects in various sectors.

Innovative Approach

• We conduct all our operations in line with the United Nations Sustainable Development Goals and aim to allocate our resources to R&D efforts.

• We strive to facilitate and develop innovative technologies that contribute to sustainable development.

• We aim to transform our organizational culture into one that is aware of the impacts of sustainability.

• Constantly measuring and evaluating the economic, environmental, and social impacts, we endeavor to minimize the negative effects of our activities.

Fair Society

• We ensure equal opportunities and prioritize social health in every sector and geography where we operate.

• We develop and implement practices that enhance occupational health and safety for our employees and stakeholders.

• We prioritize local employment to support local communities and collaborate with various institutions to create social projects.

• By increasing employee loyalty and well-being, we create conditions to make the work environment more efficient.

• We provide equal rights to our employees and potential candidates by preventing any form of discrimination.

• We support gender equality, women's empowerment, women's employment, and the qualified active participation of women at all levels.

Green Future

• We take improvement steps to achieve sectoral targets by measuring and reporting the environmental impacts of our activities.

• We constantly monitor, evaluate and implement changes in the infrastructure to increase energy efficiency.

• We review our energy consumption to increase the use of renewable energy resources.

• We develop projects that ensure the effective use of water and raise the awareness of all stakeholders and employees.

• We aim to fulfill our responsibilities by calculating, verifying and monitoring our emissions within the scope of combating climate change.

Sustainable Development Goals

As GIA, we believe that implementing SDGs is not only the responsibility of governments but also of all of us. In this regard, we advocate for the collective understanding and implementation of SDGs by companies, civil society organizations, and humanity as a whole.

We support SDGs at every stage of our value chain, focusing on expanding our social, environmental, and economic impact areas. Our dialogues with stakeholders serve the 8 Sustainable Development Goals set by national and international sector alliances in line with global standards:

Goal 5: Gender Equality
Goal 6: Clean Water and Sanitation
Goal 7: Affordable and Clean Energy
Goal 8: Decent Work and Economic Growth
Goal 9: Industry, Innovation, and Infrastructure
Goal 11: Sustainable Cities and Communities
Goal 12: Responsible Consumption and Production
Goal 13: Climate Action

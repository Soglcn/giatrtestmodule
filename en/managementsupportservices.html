As GIA, we offer a wide range of management support services to businesses and investors.

Management Support Services

Management

ANADOLU ULUSLARARASI PARK INC. is the managing company of the Anatolian International Special Industrial Zone, declared by the President's decision dated 17.05.2023 and numbered 7291.

Office rental purchase / sale

GIA guides businesses and investors in renting or purchasing suitable office spaces, and we provide support in the strategic positioning process.

Investment Advisory and Asset Management

As GIA, we guide our investors in evaluating investment opportunities in our region and support them in effectively managing their assets.

Project and Development Services

At GIA, we provide professional support to businesses in initiating, planning, and implementing their projects. We guide them through project management and evaluation processes.

Real Estate Development and Financial Solutions

We offer strategic financial consultancy to investors for financing and developing real estate projects. We provide support in investment analysis, financial planning, and risk management.

Land Development

GIA provides strategic planning and development services to businesses for evaluating and utilizing land assets efficiently.

Design and Implementation

We offer consultancy to businesses and investors in successfully implementing projects by combining aesthetics and functionality. We specialize in architecture, interior design, and related fields

Real Estate Law

GIA provides legal consultancy to businesses regarding real estate law. We offer expertise in leasing, title deeds, agreements, and regulations to ensure our clients' legal security.

Logistics and Industrial Consulting Services

We provide expert support to businesses in optimizing logistics processes, complying with industrial standards, and exploring opportunities such as industrial symbiosis.

Logistics Services

GIA offers logistics support to businesses for the effective transportation of goods and services. We provide consultancy in supply chain management, storage, transportation, and logistics strategies to enhance operational efficiency.

Incentive Desk

With our Incentive Desk unit, we track updated legislation, research the most suitable state incentives and grants for your company, and ensure you reach a conclusion quickly.

Certification Consultancy

Our company works through experienced experts to understand your business needs and offer the most suitable solutions. We provide consultancy on ISO, Green Building, rooftop and land solar energy investments, implementation of energy efficiency projects proposed against increasing energy costs and greenhouse gas emissions, helping you meet your requirements and successfully complete the certification process.

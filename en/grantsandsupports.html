Grants, Incentives and Supports

Investors are offered ready infrastructure services, the ability to utilize the sixth region investment incentives, R&D opportunities, tax reductions, VAT exemption, customs duty exemption, interest support, and income tax withholding, among many other advantages. They can benefit from the highest incentive rates and reach their goals much faster, as well as take advantage of favorable investment opportunities.

Our region is also classified as a sixth-degree incentive zone. Thus, investors will be able to benefit from many advantages such as VAT exemption, customs duty exemption, tax reductions, interest support, and income tax withholding, thanks to the highest incentive rates. They will be able to reach their goals much faster.

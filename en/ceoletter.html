CEO's Letter

Dear GIA Employees and Partners,

As the Chairman of the Board of Directors of GIA, it is with great pride and excitement that I extend a warm welcome to all of you. GIA is an initiative aimed at becoming Turkey's largest Industrial Zone and striving to achieve significant international recognition

In this private project where we will bring together foreign and domestic capital, our goal is not only to be distinguished by its size but also by its transportation networks, climatic advantages, and proximity to strategic locations.

The design of this project has been developed with consideration for our country's goals of entering the new century and green-digital transformation. We aim not just to be an industrial zone but to stand out as an international investment hub. As a company with international production and manufacturing potential, GIA aims to make significant contributions to our country's economic and industrial growth.

Throughout this process, I want to emphasize our strong commitment to sustainability and our transparent communication policy at GIA. We are committed to embracing digital transformation and endeavor to provide you with the latest company news through our website.

Our goal at GIA is to continue building a better world. I am aware of the responsibility that comes with being part of this major project, and I am eager to work alongside you all in this exciting journey. With warmest regards,

Hüseyin YARAR
Chairman of the Board, GIA
